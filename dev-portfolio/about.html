Meet the Designer

In case you haven't seen already, I'm Angel. Thanks for stopping by, if you want to know more about me, keep reading.

My journey in tech began from my best friend convincing me to take a programming class with her in high school. Once I learned you can create art with code, I was hooked. This led me to study Computer Science from the University of Memphis.

However, when I first started college, I didn't know exactly what I wanted to do in CS. It wasn't until my junior year that I found out what UX/UI design was and it has piqued my interest ever since.

One of my fondest memories in college was my time participating in the Disney College Program. As someone with a severe food allergy, I remember how great it felt to still enjoy incredible food experiences at Disney, since they have specific menus for common allergens. That sense of thoughtful inclusion stuck with me — and it’s what I want people to feel when using my designs.
