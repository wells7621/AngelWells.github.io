Meet the Designer

Growing up, I always knew I’d do something creative, like fashion design, interior design--even architecture crossed my mind. What I didn’t expect was that I’d end up designing apps through code.

I got into computer science when my best friend talked me into a programming class in high school. Once I realized you could create art with code, I was hooked.

During my junior year of college, I discovered UX/UI design,and suddenly, everything clicked. It combined all the things I loved: visual storytelling while making a real-world impact.

One of my biggest design inspirations come from theme parks. As a lifelong theme park enthusiast, I've always paid attention to the details that make an experience unforgettable. During my time in the Disney College Program, I got to explore every Disney World park and plenty of restaurants. Despite having a severe food allergy and a mild disability, I still had an amazing time.

That level of thoughtful inclusion left a mark on me. It’s exactly how I want people to feel when they use the products I help design: considered, comfortable, and completely welcome.
